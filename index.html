About Me

I am a .Net Web Developer

I am a self-taught .NET developer with 1+ years of commercial experience as a junior developer, and 6 years experience in other industries – including operating my own business. In the past 3 years, I have transitioned into the field of web development with a strong emphasis on the back end.

I initially started development in VBA whilst gaining an understanding for the fundamentals of coding.
